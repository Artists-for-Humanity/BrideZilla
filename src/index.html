Home Overview Game

Welcome to Bridezilla. Your friend is now getting married and she asked you to be her bridesmaid yay !! You decide to help her go shopping before the wedding, but there's one issue... You find out that she is a Bridezilla. In the beginning of the game the bride shouts out a demand to the bridesmaid who then has to run around to different stations depending on what the bride asked for. You must complete tasks within a certain time frame and then return to the bride with the item.
